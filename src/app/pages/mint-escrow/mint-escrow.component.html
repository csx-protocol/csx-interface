CSX tokens to Escrowed CSX

CSX tokens can be escrowed and converted into eCSX. These tokens are held for a 24-month period once Vested and during this time they will be staked to earn a share of the network's revenue. After the 24-month vesting period, the full amount of escrowed eCSX tokens can be converted back into regular CSX tokens.
